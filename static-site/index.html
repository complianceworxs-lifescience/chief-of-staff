Frequently Asked Questions

ComplianceWorxs is an AI-powered compliance intelligence platform designed for life sciences companies. We automate regulatory gap analysis, generate audit-ready documentation, and provide real-time compliance scoring to help you stay ahead of FDA, EMA, and ISO requirements.

Each customer's data is isolated in dedicated, encrypted environments. We implement SOC 2 Type II controls, end-to-end encryption, and strict access policies. Your regulatory documents and compliance data are never shared or used to train our models.

Our AI engine analyzes your documentation against current FDA, EMA, and ISO requirements using a proprietary scoring algorithm. Each gap is weighted by severity, regulatory citation, and potential audit impact. The resulting risk score provides a quantified measure of your compliance posture.

ComplianceWorxs generates comprehensive audit packages including gap analysis reports, remediation timelines, evidence trails, and executive summaries. Exports are available in PDF, Excel, and structured XML formats suitable for regulatory submissions.
